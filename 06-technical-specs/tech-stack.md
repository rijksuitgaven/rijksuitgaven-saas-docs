## Technology Evaluation Criteria

### Selection Criteria
1. **Maturity:** Stable, well-maintained, strong community
2. **Performance:** Meets performance requirements
3. **Scalability:** Scales to our needs
4. **Developer Experience:** Good documentation, tooling
5. **Cost:** Fits budget constraints
6. **Security:** Regular updates, security features
7. **Team Expertise:** Team has or can acquire skills
8. **Ecosystem:** Good integration with other tools
